My Story

Hello! My name is Baptiste I am 31 years old.
I am from France, and I live in Oregon.
My first working experience was as an Car auto body repair and painter for couple years.
I love traveling and discover different work, so I quit my job and started to travel in France having a different work experience.

I worked in south of France in a ski resort.
In the EST of France as a stand wood painter for a compagny who worked with presticious client like Chanel, Guerlain, Longchamp, Focal, DS, etc.
In the French Alpes in an hotel.

I traveled in Australia and on a cruise to go to New Zealand. This is where I met my futur husband
I came in Oregon a first time in 2019 and had to return in France.
I found a job in a door factory.
I was supposed to come back in 2020 but covid happend.
So i stayed in France and worked in the door factory but, after two years working in a door factory, I needed a change in my life.

I made the big decision to start a professional retraining, a 5 months bootcamp and became a web developper.
I wont lie and said that bootcamp was easy for me because it wasn't, in fact I left that bootcamp with doubt, regret, low confidence on myself and on my abbility to code.
the basics wasn't aquired and continue to learn without a good foundation wasn't a good idea.
After my bootcamp I didn't touch a line of code for 6 months, but I was really frustrated to just give up.
So in Februrary 2022 I bought a litle training from a French youtuber and I started from zero and learning the basics HTML, CSS, JS.
I am not a fullstack developer but I proud to what a did and not a shame to said I am a web developper junior.
today I'm happy and motivated to continue in this new way.
